                    🔁 CORE SYSTEM FLOW

   User (React App)
        ↓
   Discover sessions by:
     - shared interest
     - location radius
        ↓
   Join session
        ↓
   Go to physical venue (IRL)
        ↓
   GPS check-in
        ↓
   Shared co-presence begins
        ↓
   Work / create / collaborate (low-pressure)

        NOT:
        - social posts
        - chat messages
        - follower graphs

        BUT:
        → structured shared physical intent
